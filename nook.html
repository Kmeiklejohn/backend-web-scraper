Earn and Learn.

GET A JOB WHILE ENROLLED AT KENZIE. Kenzie Academy and Kelly Services are joining forces with their NEW Earn and Learn Program to set you on the path to a rewarding career in tech.

While you gain the skills you need to work in the high-demand tech field, you will have access to a part-time, flexible, technical support job that works with your class schedule. Best of all, you can work from home.

Our Students.

Kenzie students are driven individuals looking for an alternate path, a refreshed career or an elevated skillset that will reshape the future for themselves, their family and their community. Our students are looking for a faster way to gain the skills needed to get to work and onto a new career in tech.


 

Learn from the Best - by Doing.

Kenzie Academy is turning traditional education on its head by tearing down the wall between education and work. We believe the best way for you to develop your talent is to learn by doing.

Forget the lecture hall, engage with peers, all in a simulated business environment. Through immersive learning and a cutting-edge curriculum adapted in real time by industry professionals, Kenzie Academy delivers project-based training in person or online to prepare students with the skills and professional experience needed for high-demand tech jobs.

Democratize Tech.

Every job is becoming a technology job. Kenzie directly addresses one of the biggest obstacles to your education by removing financial barriers.

  • Earn and Learn. Get a part-time job you can do from home while enrolled through Kelly Services.

  • Income Share Agreement (ISA). Well pay for your training upfront. Youll pay us back once you have a job.

 

Get the Skills. Get the Job.

Move quickly through one- to two-year, hands-on, project-based curriculum to become a software engineer, UX engineer, or digital marketer. Get the skills and professional experience to immediately engage in the workforce.

Kenzie develops talent employers want to hire. Kenzie students emerge with the passion, skills and professional experience needed to immediately land a job. Our employer partners are looking for talent like you. Our team talks to you about your career goals, and we match you with employers ready to hire.
